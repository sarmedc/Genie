The Genie App

Simple Wishlists

Have a single place to collect all the items you've been wishing for.

Scan Barcodes

The Genie App makes it easy to add items on the spot by scanning a barcode.

Connect with friends

The Genie App lets you see your friends wishlist- no more guessing!

Keep It A Secret

When you purchase a gift for a friend, you can mark it as purchased anonymously so it doesn't ruin the surprise!
